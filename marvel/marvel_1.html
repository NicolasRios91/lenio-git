WOLVERINE

Wolverine , whose birth name is James Howlett (also known as James Logan or simply Logan )
4 is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics ,
primarily in association with the X-Men . He is a mutant possessing animal-tuned senses,
enhanced physical abilities, a powerful regenerative ability known as a healing factor,
and three retractable claws on each hand. Wolverine has been represented in
various ways as a member of the X-Men, Alpha Flight , X-Force and The Avengers..
